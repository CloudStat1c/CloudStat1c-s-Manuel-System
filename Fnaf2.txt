TOY ANIMATRONICS
----------------
- **Toy Freddy**: Slowly approaches your office; use mask if he enters.
- **Toy Bonnie**: Left hallway; flash lights and use mask when needed.
- **Toy Chica**: Right hallway; flash lights and use mask when needed.
- **Mangle**: Right vent; frequent flashlight checks prevent its advance.
- **Balloon Boy (BB)**: Left vent; disables your flashlight if he enters office.
- **The Puppet**: Must keep music box wound; neglecting it causes immediate game over.

WITHERED ANIMATRONICS
----------------------
- **Withered Freddy**: Moves slowly; mask prevents attack if he enters office.
- **Withered Bonnie**: Approaches from left side; watch left hallway closely.
- **Withered Chica**: Approaches from right; watch right hallway.
- **Withered Foxy**: Hides in Pirate Cove; check cameras often to prevent sudden sprint.
- **Withered Golden Freddy**: Rare, unpredictable; appears in office. Avoid staring at him too long; crash may occur. Use mask to survive.

GENERAL NOTES:
- Each animatronic has unique audio cues; listen carefully.
- Withereds are more aggressive at later nights.
- Multi-tasking is key: music box + mask + cameras + flashlight.
